Valenice Balace

Started her tech career as a backend engineer for a telecommunications company in Manila at 19. Later on focused on User Experience and Product Development for mobiles phones and applications.

In the present, she was recognized as part of Forbes 30 under 30 for Consumer Technology in Asia for her work as co-founder and CEO of Honesty Worldwide Ltd, Hong Kong, creating social apps like Peekawoo and their new upcoming app, Twine.

She is also Certified for Usability Design for Web and Mobile Applications from Human Factors in Boston.

Val currently holds female founders sessions in the Philippines empowering women and coaching them through Pollenizer's Lean Startup Workshop.
