Gail Tan

Gail Tan heads Communications and Public Affairs at Google Philippines and Thailand. She has been building communication strategies for over 17 years, including a ten-year stint working for two Philippine presidents. Immediately before her role at Google, Gail was the social media manager for one of President Bill Clinton's national security advisers on counter-terrorism. She holds a masters degree in Liberal Arts in Management from Harvard University and a Masters in Public Management from UP.
