Luna Cruz

Luna Cruz is the Creative Director and Co-Founder of Altitude Games, a Manila-based studio creating original, free-to-play mobile games. She started as a game writer in 2002 as part of the pioneer team that released the first Filipino-made game. Before Altitude, she previously worked with Boomzap Entertainment, creating best-selling casual adventure franchises such as Awakening and Dana Knightstone. She has also taught game design and interactive narrative at De La Salle University.
