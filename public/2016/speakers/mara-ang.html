Mara Ang

Mara graduated with a course on Culinary Arts and worked as a pastry chef for a luxury 5 star hotel but decided to ditch the world of cakes and chef coats for a life in technology, her first love.

She started to learn HTML & CSS at age 11, and eventually bought several domains and her own webspace and earns as Tech blogger. She later on joined Peekawoo as a co-founder and now heads all product development for all apps made by Honesty Worldwide Ltd, Hong Kong plus consults for various apps for other companies in Asia.
