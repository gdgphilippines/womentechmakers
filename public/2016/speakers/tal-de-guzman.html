Tal de Guzman

Tal de Guzman is the founder and main designer of Risqué. She graduated from the Ateneo de Manila University with a degree in Art Management, but later on took additional courses in Fashion Design from the School of Fashion and the Arts, and Entrepreneurship from The One School.

She has always had an affinity for local handicrafts and has made it her life's work to be a medium for transforming traditional crafts into modern designs. She dreams of growing Risqué both in local and global markets --- telling the story of the Filipino one shoe at a time.
